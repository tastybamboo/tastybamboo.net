Frequently asked questions

Is the open source version really free?

Yes. Panda CMS is open source under the BSD 3-Clause license. You can use it for personal and commercial projects at no cost. Pro adds enterprise features on top.

Can I upgrade from Open Source to Pro later?

Absolutely. Pro is designed to be a drop-in addition to your existing Panda CMS installation. Just add the gem and run migrations.

What counts as a "site"?

One production deployment of Panda CMS. Development and staging environments are free and don't count toward your license.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer significant discounts for registered non-profits and educational institutions. Contact us to discuss your needs.

What does the Pro license include?

The Pro license covers both panda-cms-pro and panda-helpdesk for one production site. You get collections, content versioning, role-based access control, REST API, content sync, website users, and a full helpdesk with ticketing system.

How does this compare to Spina CMS Pro?

Panda CMS free already includes forms, URL redirects, and a modern block editor — features that Spina charges $279 + $59/yr for. Panda Pro adds versioning, RBAC, a REST API, and a built-in helpdesk for £120/yr (inc. VAT), significantly undercutting Spina Pro while offering more features.
